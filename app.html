piano app 1

For iOS, Android, Mac and PC.

intro

I learn how to play new piano songs by watching YouTube videos with falling notes. This has a few problems.

  • The 0.25x minimum playback speed is sometimes too fast
  • The piano doesn't sound good when the video is slowed down
  • Ads

how it'll work

This is what the app looks like. Upload any MIDI file, choose the hand you want to play (left or right), use the controls to adjust the speed (as slow as 0.01x if you want!) and play along. At your ideal speed. With an un-distorted piano sound. And no ads.
