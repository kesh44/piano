How to download my MIDI files

Subscribers can download my MIDI files for free using this form.

YouTube has a feature where I can set a custom pronoun on my channel which only subscribers can see. This is a relatively new YouTube feature documented here.

  1. Go to my YouTube channel
  2. Subscribe, wait for a few seconds ⌛ then refresh the page.
  3. You will now see the pronouns listed next to my channel name. You'll need to enter this into the form to prove that you are subscribed to the channel.

I understand and respect that pronouns are an important part of people's expression and I am not exactly using this feature as intended... However this is the most reliable way of knowing whether someone really is subscribed, and I would like to make sure that is the case before I give away MIDIs which have taken many hours to create!
