Absolutely! The MIDI player works perfectly with any MIDI file, including ones from my tutorial descriptions.

Most common formats including MP3, WAV, and M4A are supported.

Can I practice at different speeds?

Yes! The MIDI player lets you adjust playback speed from very slow to full tempo - perfect for learning.

How accurate is the audio-to-MIDI conversion?

Very high accuracy on clear piano recordings. Multiple parameters let you fine-tune results.
