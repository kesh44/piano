What Type of Videos?

You can submit any falling-notes style video where the piano keys are clearly visible for the whole video. The process detects illuminated keys in the video so videos with clear notes and good picture quality work the best.

In some rare cases, a video will have too many effects, making it difficult to detect the notes being played. In these cases, a transcription will unfortunately not be available.
