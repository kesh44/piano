what is a video-to-midi request?

Have ever you watched a 'falling notes' piano video on another channel and wanted the midi, but it was either too expensive or not available? This service can convert a synthesia-style video to a midi file!

what videos can I submit?

You can submit any falling-notes style video where the piano keys are clearly visible for the whole video. This service works by detecting the illuminated keys on the piano, so videos with good picture quality and frame rate work the best.

In some rare cases, a video will have properties that make it difficult to detect the notes being played. In these cases, a midi transcription will unfortunately not be available.
