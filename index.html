FAQs

I use a piano app called Synthesia to make videos. The MIDIs are recorded on my piano which is a Yamaha P45, and I occasionally use a Novation Launchkey 25. MIDIs are recorded in the music app Reaper. The piano sound I use is Addictive Keys by XLN Audio.

If any MIDIs are missing from my store, please tell me in the YouTube comments and I will add them.

If a song is missing, it's because I don't have a license to sell it (yet). I can only sheet music when I have an agreement in place with the retailer (Musicnotes) and the song publisher. If there's sheet music you want to download, please tell me in the YouTube comments and I will prioritise getting a license for it.
