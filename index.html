what is a video-to-midi request?

Have ever you watched a 'falling notes' piano video and wanted the midi, but it was either too expensive or not available? That's what this service is for! You can convert a 'falling notes' video to a midi file!

Please remember: all midis on my channel are FREE. If you would like one of my midis, drop me an email at freepianomidis@gmail.com, and I will send it to you! The video-to-midi service described here is for getting midis from videos on other channels!

what videos can I submit?

You can submit any 'falling notes' video where the piano keys are clearly visible for the whole video - see the pictures for examples. The conversion works by detecting the illuminated keys in the video, so videos with good picture quality and high frame rate work the best.

In some rare cases, a video will have properties that make it difficult to detect the notes being played. In these cases, a midi transcription will unfortunately not be available.

what are the steps?

1. Complete the form below

2. I will transcribe the video to midi and email you the audio (.mp3) of the transcription

3. If you are satisfied with it, proceed to payment (via PayPal)

4. You will then recieve the MIDI file via email
