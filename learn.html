An easier way to learn YouTube tutorials

Learning from Synthesia tutorials is a great way for people to learn new songs quickly. But the YouTube player is not the best if you are constantly rewinding because you are trying to replay a section of the video. That's where my app comes in! I have made a YouTube video player which adds useful controls for learning tutorials. So you can easily:

  • Rewind 5 or 10 seconds
  • Rewind to a specific time
  • Quickly adjust speed controls

And the best part? This works for ANY YouTube video! So you can learn tutorials from other creators, or for other instruments too.
